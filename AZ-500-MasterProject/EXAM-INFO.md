# About the AZ-500 Exam

Everything you need to know before you register and sit the AZ-500 exam.

---

## Quick Facts

| Field | Detail |
|-------|--------|
| **Exam Name** | Microsoft Azure Security Technologies |
| **Exam Code** | AZ-500 |
| **Certification Earned** | Microsoft Certified: Azure Security Engineer Associate |
| **Cost** | $165 USD (varies by country — always verify at time of booking) |
| **Passing Score** | 700 out of 1000 |
| **Total Questions** | 40–60 questions (may include 1 performance-based lab with ~12 sub-tasks) |
| **Exam Duration** | 150 minutes (non-native English speakers may request up to 180 minutes) |
| **Proctoring** | Pearson VUE (online or in-person test centre) |
| **Renewal** | Required annually via a FREE online renewal assessment on Microsoft Learn |

---

## Question Types

You will see a mix of these question formats:

- **Multiple-choice** — pick one correct answer from four options
- **Multi-select** — pick two or more correct answers
- **Drag-and-drop** — match items or order a sequence of steps
- **Case studies** — read a scenario, then answer several related questions
- **Performance-based labs** — complete real tasks inside a live Azure environment

> Performance-based labs appear at the end of the exam. They take more time. Budget at least 30 minutes for them.

## Prerequisites

There are no official prerequisites to sit AZ-500.

However, **AZ-104 knowledge is strongly recommended**. AZ-500 assumes you already understand core Azure services, resource management, and the Azure portal. If you are new to Azure, study AZ-104 first.

## How to Register Step by Step

1. Go to the official certification page:
   **https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/credentials/certifications/azure-security-engineer/**

2. Click the **"Schedule exam"** button on that page.

3. Sign in with your Microsoft account. Create one for free if you do not have one.

4. You will be redirected to **Pearson VUE**, the exam delivery platform.

5. Choose your exam format:
   - **Online proctored** — take the exam at home or in your office. You need a webcam and a quiet room.
   - **Test centre** — take the exam at a Pearson VUE test centre near you.

6. Select your **preferred date and time**.

7. Enter your **payment details**. The cost is approximately $165 USD. The exact price depends on your country.

8. Review your booking. Confirm it.

9. You will receive a **confirmation email** from Pearson VUE. Save it.

10. On exam day, follow the instructions in the confirmation email to check in.

> **Tip:** Microsoft offers discounts for students and people who have failed a previous attempt. Check the certification page for current offers.

## What to Expect on Exam Day

### Before the Exam

- Arrive at the test centre 15 minutes early. For online exams, start the check-in process 30 minutes early.
- Bring a **government-issued photo ID**. Your name must exactly match the name in your Pearson VUE account.
- For online exams, run the **Pearson VUE system check** the day before. It tests your webcam, microphone, and internet connection.
- Close all other applications on your computer before the online exam begins.
- Clear your desk. No papers, no phones, no food (for online). You may have water in a clear glass.

### During the Exam

- Read every question twice before answering.
- Watch for **trigger words**: "always", "never", "most", "least", "first". They change the correct answer.
- Flag questions you are unsure about. Come back to them.
- Do not spend more than 2–3 minutes on any single question in the multiple-choice section.
- **Performance-based labs come last.** Do not rush the multiple-choice section — but do not linger either.
- In the labs, work systematically. The sub-tasks build on each other.
- You **cannot go back** to multiple-choice questions once you enter the lab section.

### After the Exam

- Your score appears on screen immediately after you finish.
- You receive a pass or fail result. You will see your score and a breakdown by domain.
- If you pass, your certification appears on your Microsoft Learn profile within 24–48 hours.
- If you fail, you must wait **24 hours** before retaking. After a second or third failure, the wait increases to **14 days**.

## How to Renew

Your AZ-500 certification is valid for **one year** from the date you pass.

To renew, you do **not** need to sit the full exam again.

### Renewal Steps

1. **Watch for the reminder email.** Microsoft sends you an email 6 months before expiry and again at 60 days before.

2. Go to your **Microsoft Learn profile** and open your certification.

3. Click **"Renew certification"**.

4. Complete the **free online renewal assessment** on Microsoft Learn.
   - It is not proctored.
   - You can use Microsoft Learn documentation during the assessment.
   - No time limit.
   - You need a passing score to renew.

5. If you pass, your certification is extended by **one year** from the original expiry date.

6. If you do not pass, you can retake the renewal assessment after **24 hours**.

> **Key point:** Renewal is completely free. You do not pay again. You just need to pass the online assessment.

> **Tip:** Renew early. Do not wait until the final week. If you miss the window, you must sit and pay for the full exam again.
